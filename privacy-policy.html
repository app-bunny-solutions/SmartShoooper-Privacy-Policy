1. Summary

Smart Shopper helps users create and manage shopping lists, track purchases, receive reminders, and discover relevant product suggestions. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2. Information We Collect

We collect three categories of information: (A) information you provide directly, (B) information collected automatically, and (C) aggregated or derived information.

A. Information you provide

  • Account details: If you choose to create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and a password.
  • Shopping data: the shopping lists, items, quantities, categories, notes, and any photos or attachments you add to lists.
  • Purchase history: purchases you record in the App (amounts, dates, payment mode, vendor name) and optional receipts you attach.
  • Communications: messages you send to support or feedback forms.

B. Information collected automatically

  • Device & usage data: device model, OS version, app version, crash logs, and how you interact with the App (pages visited, features used).
  • Identifiers for advertising & analytics: advertising IDs (Google Advertising ID / IDFA) and anonymized identifiers for analytics providers when enabled.
  • Performance metrics: anonymized timing, speed and error reports collected to improve the App.

C. Derived and aggregated information

We may aggregate or analyze data (for example: most popular grocery items across users) and use it in non-identifying ways to improve the App and services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the App (save your shopping lists and purchase history).
  • To personalize content and suggestions (e.g., frequently purchased items, category suggestions).
  • To send optional reminders and push notifications (low-stock alerts, shopping reminders) when you opt in.
  • To process in-app purchases or subscription billing via app stores (Apple App Store / Google Play).
  • To detect and fix technical issues and improve performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests by public authorities.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: trusted third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting, email delivery, payment processing). They only receive the data needed to perform their services and are contractually bound to protect your information.
  • Legal requests: if required by law or to comply with a valid legal process, or to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, sale of assets, or bankruptcy, user data may be transferred as part of the transaction (with notice where required by law).

5. In-App Advertising & Analytics

Smart Shopper may display advertising and uses analytics tools to understand usage patterns. These third parties may collect information about your device and interactions to provide interest-based ads or analytics. Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID or IDFA) may be used for ad personalization. You can limit ad tracking or reset advertising identifiers via your device settings.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

As a mobile app, Smart Shopper stores data locally on your device (preferences, cached lists) and may use cookies or similar local mechanisms in any web views. Clearing the app data will remove locally stored content unless you have synced it to a cloud account (if account sync is available).

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide the App, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and for internal analytics. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights under data protection laws. These may include:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal exceptions).
  • Portability: request a machine-readable copy of certain information you provided.
  • Opt-out of targeted ads: adjust advertising settings on your device or use the opt-out options of ad partners.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the contact details below. We will respond in accordance with applicable law.

9. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Examples include encrypted communication (TLS) and access controls. However, no system is 100% secure — please take care when choosing what to store in the App.

10. Children

Smart Shopper is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us and we will promptly delete the information as required by law.

11. International Transfers
